About Babe ART

Babe ART is a database management system for fertility treatment clinics. Our software relates patients, treatment, laboratory data and outcomes together. Babe is developed and supported by European and Finnish fertility specialists since 1996.

Integrated Modules

We focus on developing tools that support the clinic daily operations, simplify the documentation process, and increase staff productivity. Babe software is presented in a practical modular system, making it easy to integrate and navigate between modules. Following are some main modules:

Registry management


Registry management

Easily manage patient information, register new patient, organize and access to the list of donors, patients.

Laboratory management


Laboratory management

A complete package covers all of the key requirements to manage an Assisted Reproductive Technology clinic. Some highlighted features include Sperm Storage, Egg Bank, Cells Store, Embryo Storage, etc.

Fertility treatments management


Fertility treatment management

All fertility treatment performed in the clinic are accessible in this module. Treatment forms are issued in Finnish, English, and Swedish, with the possibility of creating new language as desired.

Embryology module


Embryology module

New access to the history of frozen and stored embryos. The separation of embryology culture for IVF and ICSI cells is useful for embryologists and clinicians.

Donor module


Donor module

An independent module for the management of donors, along with user restrictions defined by each clinic. This module includes information of clinic workflow from the donor registration stage to the end result.

Other clinic management features


Other clinic management features

Maintenance features such as invoicing, scheduling, document management are designed to integrate with the whole system, and to support your smooth working days.

Customizable, international solution

Babe Manager, the homepage where users can be directed to different modules of Babe, allows addition or activation of needed modules. Clinics are free to customise images, logo, and contacts. The decision is in your hands!
